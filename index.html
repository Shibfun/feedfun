2. Bet 1000000000 FEED to join (1 bet per round).
3. When 10 bets are in, a random winner is picked.
4. Winner gets 8000000000 FEED, losers each get 10 FAME.
5. 2000000000 FEED is burned.
6. New round starts automatically!
